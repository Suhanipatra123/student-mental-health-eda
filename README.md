📊 Student Mental Health Analysis (EDA)
🧠 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing student mental health using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) techniques. The goal is to identify key factors affecting depression, anxiety, and stress levels among students.

❗ Problem Statement

Students face multiple challenges such as academic pressure, financial stress, and social isolation, which impact their mental well-being.

This project aims to use data analysis to understand which factors most strongly influence student mental health.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze mental health data using EDA
Identify relationships between academic, social, and lifestyle factors
Detect patterns and trends
Find key factors influencing depression
Provide meaningful insights and recommendations

🔍 Key Insights
📈 Academic workload increases depression
😟 Anxiety is strongly correlated with depression
💸 Financial concerns increase stress
🧍 Isolation leads to higher depression
🤝 Social relationships reduce stress
🏃 Sports engagement helps reduce anxiety
📊 Total score reflects overall mental health
👥 Group Analysis

Postgraduate students show slightly higher levels of depression and stress compared to undergraduate students.

📌 Feature Importance

Top factors affecting depression:

Anxiety
Isolation
Academic pressure
Financial concerns
✅ Conclusion

EDA revealed that academic pressure and anxiety are the major contributors to depression, while social interaction and physical activity help reduce stress.
